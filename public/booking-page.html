How to Schedule Appointments

Dietitians are experts in the art and science of nutrition to help you meet your nutrition needs

How to Get Started

Step1

Contact us to set up an appointment through one of the options:

Option 1   
Call our office on 012 662 0302 (select option 3).

Option 2
Schedule a consultation with our virtual availability and booking app.

Click here to book with Shani
Click here to book with Jason

Option 3
Send us an email with your query or booking to info@pretoriadietitian.co.za
Please arrive 5 minutes before your appointment to complete the required forms.

Step2

During your consultation, your dietitians shall ask you what your nutritional goals are. From there we discuss those goals and how to practically achieve them with a nutritional plan.

We do an in-depth nutritional assessment, do the Inbody 120 scan (if needed) and will explain and discuss the necessary nutritional concepts with you.

If a meal plan is needed then we develop an individualized meal plan and email it to you.

Step3

We are a cash practice so payments will be made via cash or card after the consultation.
‍
A statement will be emailed within 3 working days, which you can send to your medical aid to claim if you choose to do so.

Please be aware that some medical aids do not reimburse the full amount for the services provided.
